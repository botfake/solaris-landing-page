About COUNCIL

The Student Council is a students' body whose main purpose is to represent and promote the interests of the students of Jain College CGS. The Student Council was formed in the year 2015.
The council is responsible for the organisation of the college’s intra and inter collegiate fests. Given this free reign, the members of the council can experience management outside the classroom too, working for and pushing initiatives that can make a difference to students, the institute and also the environment we are a part of. The Student Council has organised 4 national level fests alongside multiple intra collegiate fests.
