Solaris Model

Solaris is a one-of-a-kind management fest, organised by The Student Council Jain College-Vasavi Campus. During the course of the event, the participant's understanding of the business world will be put to test. The participants will be representing the top level management of an organisation and will deal with real life scenarios as a representative of the organisation. Solaris is an attempt at giving undergraduate student a corporate experience in a safe environment that contributes to their personal and professional growth. A contingent will consist of 5 members who will portray the following managerial positions:

CEO (Chief Executive Officer):

The Chief Executive Officer is the highest-ranking person in a company, ultimately responsible for taking managerial decisions. This person in your team gets the cumulative blame or praise for every decision you take.


CFO(Chief Financial Officer):

Chief Financial officer is a senior executive with the responsibility of the financial affairs of a company or other institutions. This money whizz better go the extra mile for you to stay rich!


Head of Marketing:

The responsibilities of the Head of Marketing include developing plans to help establish your brand. If you're a skilled Marketing strategist and are able to inspire the world to invest in you, we'd like to meet you.


CCO(Chief Communications Officer):

Chief Communications Officer (CCO) is the head of communications, public relations, and/or public affairs in an organization. Typically, the CCO of a corporation reports to the chief executive officer (CEO). This person in your team better know how to deal with pressure and spin a tale.


ODM(Organisational Development Manager):

The Organizational Development Manager plans, develops, implements and administers development and policies for company employees. The ODM coordinates with the CCO and CEO in dealing with any internal or external issues of the company.
